About us

Building Trust

Through Metal

Hello and welcome to Locsmyth.com. We are excited to offer a variety of quality products for natural hair, locks, and coils. Our website is dedicated to you and your specific hair care needs. Locksmyth.com is the brainchild of CEO and founder J. D. Howard. If you have ever had a frustrating shopping experience finding products for your locs and coils, look no further. We have it all on this site. You may browse and buy your favorite products from your favorite retailers on your own time, at your own pace with the convenience of online shopping. We care about your shopping and browsing experience. And our goal is to provide access to national, international, and cottage industry product lines for the best variety and selection to suit every taste. We have products that large retailers do not have. We built this platform for you, a very discerning customer with Afro textured hair. This site is dedicated specifically to Afro hair textures. Thank you for visiting our site. We aim to provide the best care for the hair we wear. Happy shopping!
